Welcome! This blog covers artificial intelligence — from foundational concepts
like neural networks and training, to the latest developments in large language
models, agents, and applied AI systems.

Posts here are written as plain Markdown files in `content/posts`, so adding a
new post is as simple as dropping in a new `.md` file with a frontmatter block.

Stay tuned for more posts on how modern AI systems are built and used.
